Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 15-Feb-2026

SSL Public Key Inspector ("the App") respects your privacy.

1. Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

The App does not use analytics, tracking tools, advertising SDKs, or third-party data collection services.

2. Network Usage

When a domain name is entered, the App performs a secure TLS connection to retrieve publicly available SSL/TLS certificate information from the specified server.

No personal data is transmitted to any third party.

No certificate data is stored after the session ends.

All processing occurs locally on your device.

3. File Access

The App allows users to export SSL/TLS certificates to a location selected by the user.

The App only accesses files or folders explicitly selected by the user and does not access other areas of the system.

4. Data Storage

The App does not store any personal data.

Any exported certificate files are saved only to the location chosen by the user.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, updates will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.
